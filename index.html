Our Programs

A comprehensive curriculum that challenges, inspires, and prepares every student.

🎒

Pre-Kindergarten

In our Pre-Kindergarten program, children aged 3-4 years explore the world around them through sensory experiences, hands-on activities, and social interactions. Our experienced teachers guide them in developing essential skills, such as communication, fine motor control, and social skills.

📚

Elementary

Our Elementary program serves children in grades K-5, providing a challenging and engaging curriculum that incorporates trilingual education. Students develop their language skills, mathematical concepts, and scientific knowledge while exploring their interests and passions.

🏫

Middle/Upper

In our Middle/Upper program, students in grades 6-8 deepen their understanding of core subjects, develop critical thinking skills, and explore their interests through electives and extracurricular activities. Our experienced faculty guides them in preparing for high school and beyond.
