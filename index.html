Our Programs

A comprehensive curriculum that challenges, inspires, and prepares every student.

🎒

Pre-Kindergarten

Our Pre-Kindergarten program is designed for children aged 3-5, providing a nurturing environment for early learning and socialization. Through play-based activities and language immersion, our students develop essential skills in language, math, and social-emotional intelligence.

📚

Elementary

Our Elementary program serves students from grades K-5, offering a challenging and engaging curriculum that combines language immersion with hands-on learning. Our students develop a strong foundation in language arts, mathematics, science, and the arts, while also exploring their creativity and interests.

📝

Middle/Upper

Our Middle and Upper School programs serve students from grades 6-8, providing a rigorous and personalized education that prepares students for the next phase of their academic journey. Through a combination of language immersion and project-based learning, our students develop critical thinking, problem-solving, and leadership skills.
